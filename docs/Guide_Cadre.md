# Cadre — Platform Guide

You are a **Cadre** — a field officer who submits transport requests for review. Your job is simple: tell the platform you need something picked up and delivered, and let the dispatcher handle the rest.

---

## 1. Sign in

1. Open the app in your browser.
2. Enter your **cadre email** and **password** → **Sign In**.

> _Screenshot: login screen_

You'll land on **My Tasks** — the list of every request you've submitted.

---

## 2. The two pages you'll use

| Page | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Add Task** | Submit a new pickup-and-delivery request |
| **My Tasks** | See your submissions and their approval status |

---

## 3. Submit a new task

Click **Add Task** in the sidebar.

The form is short:

- **Title** (optional) — a short label, e.g. *"Documents to Hydra"*. If you leave it empty, the system auto-generates one from the addresses.
- **Pickup address** — start typing. **Pick a real suggestion from the dropdown** (very important — don't just type, the click is what attaches the GPS).
- **Dropoff address** — same way.
- **Date** — when this task should be done.
- **Time** — preferred pickup time.
- **Priority** — **Normal** or **Urgent**. Use Urgent only when something genuinely needs to bypass the queue.

Click **Save**.

The task is now in the system with status **Pending Approval**. The dispatcher will see it on their side and either approve or reject it.

> _Screenshot: Add Task form_

---

## 4. Track your tasks

Click **My Tasks** in the sidebar. You see a table of every request you've submitted, with three statuses possible:

| Status | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Pending Approval** | Submitted; the dispatcher hasn't reviewed it yet. You can still **edit** or **delete** it. |
| **Approved** | The dispatcher accepted it; it'll be assigned to a driver in the next plan. **You cannot edit or delete an approved task** — call the dispatcher if it needs to change. |
| **Rejected** | The dispatcher refused the request. You can delete it and submit a new one if needed. |

> _Screenshot: My Tasks list with mixed statuses_

### Edit a pending task
1. Click the **pencil** icon on the row.
2. Change what's wrong; save.

### Delete a pending or rejected task
1. Click the **trash** icon on the row.
2. Confirm the prompt.

---

## 5. After your task is approved

You don't need to do anything else. The lifecycle from your perspective:

```
You submit  →  Dispatcher approves  →  Optimizer assigns it to a driver  →  Driver does it  →  Done
```

The driver gets the SMS with the time and address; the dispatcher monitors execution. You'll see the row stay in your list with status **Approved** until the day passes.

| You want to… | Go to | What you click |
|---|---|---|
| Submit a new pickup/delivery | Add Task | Save |
| See whether your request was approved | My Tasks | (just look at the status badge) |
| Fix a typo in a pending task | My Tasks | Pencil icon |
| Cancel a request before it's approved | My Tasks | Trash icon |
| Cancel an already-approved task | (call the dispatcher) | — |

---

## 7. Tips

- **Always pick a suggestion from the address dropdown.** If you just type and don't click a result, the GPS coordinates won't be saved and your request will fail validation.
- **Don't mark everything Urgent.** Urgent tasks bump other tasks down the queue; if everything is urgent, nothing is.
- **Submit early** when you can. The earlier the dispatcher sees a request, the more likely it gets fitted into the day's plan without disrupting other deliveries.

---

## 8. What you do **not** do

- You don't pick which driver gets the task — the optimizer chooses.
- You don't see the route or the live map.
- You don't approve or reject tasks (only the dispatcher does).
- You don't communicate directly with drivers.

If something goes wrong (the wrong address was filled, the task is no longer needed), edit it while it's still pending or call the dispatcher if it's already approved.
